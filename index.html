Process
How It Works
Four steps from nested patient archives to clean, consolidated records — entirely in your browser.
01
Upload Master ZIP
Master ZIP contains a folder with individual patient ZIPs — one ZIP per patient, named after each patient.
02
Auto-Extract Patient ZIPs
Each patient's ZIP is opened automatically and all PDF documents inside are collected for merging.
03
Download Both ZIPs
Get a flat ZIP (all merged PDFs at root) and a structured ZIP (patient folders with one PDF each).
